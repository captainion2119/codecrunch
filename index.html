About the Codecrunch Hackathon


Attention B.N.M.I.T. students! Do you dream of pushing the boundaries of technology and leaving your mark on the world? Look no further than the Code Crunch Hackathon! This electrifying event invites you to showcase your skills and ingenuity in a collaborative environment. Assemble your team of up to 3 fellow B.N.M.I.T. brainiacs and tackle thrilling challenges in cutting-edge fields like artificial intelligence, image processing, and signal processing. Not only will you gain invaluable experience and network with like-minded individuals, but you'll also have the chance to win lucrative cash prizes in both the qualifying round and the grand finale. So, what are you waiting for? Register now and prepare to code your way to victory!

Win Exciting Cash Prizes:


  • First Place: ₹15000/-

  • Second Place: ₹10000/-

  • Third Place: ₹5000/-

About the Hackathon

Codecrunch is a MATLAB based Hackathon, designed to touch the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Signal Processing and other such cool tech challenges. join in and show off your skills and win exciting cash prizes. Problem solving and coding skills are the only requirements to participate in this event.
